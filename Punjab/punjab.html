Lahore:

Lahore is the capital and historically and culturally the most important city of Punjab, it is also called the heart of Pakistan. Situated on the banks of river Ravi this ancient city has a long history and a very rich culture and heritage. It has so much to offer to a visitor that we have dealt with the beautiful and historically important places in a separate article on the same website. This is a city with many historic monuments and gardens from the days of Mughals and the British Raj. You can visit the family entertainment places in lahore.

Muree:

Queen of the mountains Murree, is the most popular hill station not only in Punjab but in whole Pakistan. Hundreds of thousands of tourists flock to this beautiful hill station, situated at an elevation of over 2,000 meters. Murree is a bustling town and provides all kinds of attractions for tourists, including hotels, restaurants and a variety of shopping opportunities. Its forest covered lush green mountains present an enchanting scene in summer. And in winter when it receives snowfall the snowcapped mountains give the area an air of a fairyland. Being close to Islamabad makes it easily accessible, which makes it very popular among tourists. Murree has many places of interest, for details visit this page about the best places in Murree.
